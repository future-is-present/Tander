What we do


Privacy

Keep your privacy with our encrypted system. Blockchain technology allows large number of interactions to be codified and carried out in a way that greatly increases reliability, removes business and political risks associated with the process being managed by a central entity, and reduces the need for trust.


Decentralised

Decentralised platform and open source code in order to not provide with more power to any of the participants in Tander. It avoids fraud prevention through the use of tokens and there is not a third party that holds all of the data.


Low fees

No fees for items below 20€.

What

We leverage the ethereum smart contracts to exchange any item that you have with some people nearby. You will pay the product with your phone when meeting the other person and after see the product.Smart contracts are the bridge on blockchain to transfer any item in our platform to another user without any third party needed.
